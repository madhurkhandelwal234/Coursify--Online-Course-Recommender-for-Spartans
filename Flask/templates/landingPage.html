Intelligent Course Suggetions

Want to plan the courses according to technologies you want to learn. Coursify is just for you. Choose your courses! We will do the enrollment condition checks.

Confused after flipping through pages on web. Not sure about when can you take the course. We are there for you. You can create a course plan according to your interests. If there is a pre-requisite or co-requisite condition check involved for enrolling in a subject, we will tell you. Coursify will check the requirements and tell you that your choice is invalid.

Summary

Coursify aims to make the course selection process less stressful for students by providing them all the required information at one place. Coursify is one stop that provides students information on courses, professors and course enrollment conditions.
